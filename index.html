Features

Vectorization that actually
gets the details right

Most tools trace blindly. Vectorify's AI understands your image — curves, color regions, fine lines and all.

🧠

AI-Powered Path Detection

Our neural engine analyses shapes, gradients, and edges — not just pixel borders — producing paths that look hand-drawn by a designer.

🔍

Infinite Scalability

The resulting SVG looks perfect at any size — from a 16px favicon to a 20-foot billboard. No pixelation, ever.

🎨

Colour-Aware Tracing

Preserves exact colour palettes, gradients, and transparency. Choose how many colour layers to include for clean flat art or rich full-colour output.

⚙️

Advanced Path Controls

Tune smoothing, corner tolerance, speckle removal, and stroke detection. Or let Vectorify decide — auto mode is magic.

🚀

Batch Processing

Vectorize hundreds of logos, icons, or product images simultaneously via the dashboard or REST API. Built for production workflows.

🔌

API & Integrations

Plug into Figma, Adobe Illustrator, or your own pipeline with our clean REST API and official SDKs for Python, Node, and PHP.

Process

Three steps to vector-perfect

No Illustrator degree required. Upload, tweak (optionally), and download.

01 📤

Upload Your Image

Drop any raster image — JPG, PNG, WebP, BMP, GIF or TIFF. Up to 50 MB, any resolution.

02 ✨

AI Vectorizes Instantly

03 🎛️

Tune & Refine

04 ⬇️

Download & Ship

Export as SVG, PDF, EPS, DXF, or PNG at any DPI. Ready for print, web, or CNC.
